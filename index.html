Frequently asked questions

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans, including PPO and HMO networks, as well as Medicare. If you're unsure, call us at +1 (555) 123-4567 and we'll verify your coverage before your visit.

How soon can I be seen?

Most patients get a same-day or next-day appointment. For urgent concerns outside clinic hours, our 24/7 care line connects you with a clinician any time.

Do you offer video visits?

Yes. Many primary care and mental wellness appointments can be done by secure video visit from home — just choose "Video visit" when you book.

Can the whole family be seen at BlueHaven?

Absolutely. Our family medicine team cares for every age, from newborns to seniors, and we can coordinate specialist care on-site so you don't have to travel between offices.
